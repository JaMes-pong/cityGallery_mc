--- Our Story ---

We not dream the dream! We are the team that create Colmar City and Zephyr City. Our team have 3 members. We all are friends in school before. We three also have a dream want to achieve. So we use over 3 years to achieve our dream.We believe that we can build up a huge and most detail city in Minecraft. So we start to plan and build the city in Minecraft.

First city that we build is Colmar City. Colmar City is the city which we use most of time on it. The size also is unprecedented huge that we built before. We start to build it since 2016. Until 2018 December, we decide to stop this project first. It is because we think that we are necessary to re-plan Colmar future. So we temporary stop it. But we already have a schedule to re-launch Colmar project.

Then, our team start to plan a new city in 2019 called Zephyr City. Zephyr City is brand new city. The style or city planning is totally different by Colmar City. Zephyr City is reference Hong Kong. So in Zephyr we prepare many element that about Hong Kong, including the street name, building name. We want to achieve well-planning in Zephyr. Since we have a experience of planning Colmar. We hope that Zephyr City is a successful city in the future.
